# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the Lager project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public space where an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at hello@lagerdata.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent consequences as determined by the project leadership.
